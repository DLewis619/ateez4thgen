Formed on October 24th 2018, ATEEZ is an 8 member kpop group under KQ Entertainment. Debuting with their first album “Treasure Ep.1” the boys had quickly gained popularity. Despite where they are today the members had to face many hard challenges. At the time when they debuted the company they joined was relatively new, this means the company didn't have much money, so because of this, alot of people overlooked the talent of these very talented young men. They were mistreated, ignored, and hated for no reason at all. This didn’t stop the boys from thriving.
