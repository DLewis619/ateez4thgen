The name ATEEZ is an abbreviation for “A TEEnager Z”. ATEEZ refers to a group of teenagers who are willing to do everything from A to Z. The official fandom name of ATEEZ is ATINY, which is a combination of the words ATEEZ + DESTINY. ATINY means that ATEEZ and the fans were meant to meet. The group consists of 8 members. Kim Hongjoong - leader, lead rapper, vocalist . Park Seonghwa - lead vocalist, rapper, visual. Jeong Yunho - main dancer, vocalist, rapper, center. Kang Yeosang - lead dancer, vocalist, visual. Choi San - lead vocalist, lead dancer, face of the group. Song Mingi - main rapper, lead dancer. Jung WooYoung - main dancer, vocalist, visual.
